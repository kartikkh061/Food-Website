Pizza

faqs

Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

"We are open daily to serve you the finest flavors of India. Join us for lunch from 12:00 PM to 3:30 PM, or dinner from 7:00 PM to 11:00 PM. Reservations are recommended for weekend dining."

What do i get my refund?

"Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days via the original payment method. For orders placed through third-party apps, please initiate the request through their respective platforms."

How long it will take food to arrive?

"Quality takes time, but we move with precision. Because every dish is prepared fresh to order, you can typically expect your meal to arrive within 15 to 25 minutes. We believe the perfect blend of spices is worth the wait."

Does your restaurant has both veg and non veg?

"We serve a wide variety of veg and non-veg delicacies. From rich paneer specialties to succulent meat curries, there is something for everyone at our table."

What is cost of food delivery?

"Our delivery fee starts at $3.50 and varies depending on your distance. We also offer free delivery on orders above $25—the perfect excuse to add an extra side of Garlic Naan!"

Who is eligible for pro membership?

"Anyone can join! Simply create an account on our website or app to start earning points and unlock exclusive member-only rewards and discounts."
